S O G I E the What's and Why's

SOGIE:

Sexual Orientation, Gender, Identity, and Expression

The proper education of SOGIE, or Sexual Orientation and Gender Identity Expression, has been one of the most pressing issues in the Philippines. This has resulted to the miseducation of Filipino citizens and the misinformed people bashing others based on their SOGIE. The Sogie Bill, currently being passed, has been repeatedly bashed and degraded by using inaccurate facts and wrong information.


This website aims to inform the miseducated about SOGIE, what it means, and what it stands for, as well as the real and accurate facts about Sogie Bill. The goal of this website is to fix the issue of misinformation to broaden the perspective of close-minded Filipinos which will lead to the acceptance of people outside the norm.

SOGIE: Meaning and Definition

SOGIE is a term that covers a wide description which leads to easy misinformation due to rumors, gossips, and fake news brought up by social media. This site aims to clear misconceptions brought up by society.

SOGIE Bill: Everything You Need To Know

The Sogie Bill, proposed by Congresswoman Roman and Ex-Congresswoman Bag-Ao, raises confusion for Filipinos, as the newly presented law contradicts some of their beliefs as a person. This site aims to broaden their perspectives and clear all misconceptions.
