SOGIE Bill:

A Chance for Change

Sogie Bill is a bill that yearns to make the rights of cis and non-cis people’s rights equal. It is to prevent unfair choices against marginalized sectors and in this case, the LGBTQIA+ community. Unfair choices in this context means that people will have a certain bias against people who have different SOGIE from other than their own preferences.
